## 📌 Overview

Cairo Privacy Check helps users review privacy-related settings in Windows
in a clear and organized way. The program scans over 45 privacy settings
related to permissions, data sharing, and system options — presenting
results with clear explanations of how each setting may impact your
personal data, without requiring technical knowledge.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ Comprehensive scan of over 45 Windows privacy settings
- ✅ Clear explanation of each setting's impact on privacy
- ✅ Apply settings individually or with a single click
- ✅ Real-time status updates during scanning and configuration
- ✅ Exportable reports to monitor privacy status over time
- ✅ Administrator access support when required
- ✅ Lightweight and does not affect system stability
- ✅ Works completely offline — no internet required
- ✅ 100% Free — no fees or subscriptions

| Category | Details |

| **Activity Tracking** | Usage and activity history settings |

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Download and Install** the software on your device
2. **Open the software** — the main screen shows your privacy status
3. **Click Start Scan** to perform a full privacy settings scan
4. **Monitor Progress** — stop the scan anytime if needed
5. **Review Results** — view settings with risk levels and recommendations
6. **Apply Changes** — individually or use the one-click option
7. **Verify Changes** — confirm applied changes and review final status
8. **Export Report** — save scan results for later review
